The Craft of Persona Curation

Why English Works Best


Language models store concepts, not words.

But they're trained predominantly on English text.


Prompts in English → more predictable results
even when the output is in another language.


From the Recipe Curator:
"Match the User's Language — respond in the language the user writes in."

The persona is in English. The output adapts to the user.

Practical tip for this multilingual audience. Write your persona in English, let the AI handle the output language. The model can juggle languages perfectly well — but the instruction layer is most reliable in English because that's where the training data is densest.
