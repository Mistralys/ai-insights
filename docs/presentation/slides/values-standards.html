Together: the AI makes judgment calls, not just follows commands.

Per-meal target: 2-3 color groups. Per-week: all 6 must appear. The AI checks this automatically on every recipe and flags gaps. This is the key takeaway: equipment gives situational awareness, rainbow eating gives quality standards. Together, they let the AI make judgment calls. This is the difference between a prompt and a persona.
